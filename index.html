Microfilm For Disney

This is the Disney "Duffy's Friends" microfilm series that I worked on. I worked with six other team members to write the script, shoot, and edit the film. I was mainly responsible for scripting the subplots for the content of the film. I had to think about the order of the characters' appearances, how the character would interact with each other, and choose the scenery to make the film vibrant and full of Disney fantasy. I also assisted with the filming and editing, and the whole project took 21 days in total.
